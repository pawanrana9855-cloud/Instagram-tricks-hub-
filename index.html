👑 Samarth Special Secret
Yeh ek exclusive VIP feature hai jo aapke account ki reach ko boost karne ke liye design kiya gaya hai. Is trick se aap free views aur instant visibility pa sakte hain.
💡 Trick 1: Quality Uploads
Hamesha high-quality media content upload karein. Settings mein jaakar 'Upload at Highest Quality' option ko hamesha ON rakhein taaki algorithm reels ko dabaye na.
💡 Trick 2: Account Security
Apne account ko hack hone se bachane ke liye Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) ko turant chalu karein aur third-party apps ka access hata dein.
💡 Trick 3: Feed Clean Up
Sare inactive following aur unedited/spam tags ko clean karein. Clean feed hone se algorithm aapke naye content ko zyada logo tak push karta hai.
💡 Trick 4: Reels Viral Secret
Trending audios ka sahi use karein aur reel upload karne ke pehle 30 minutes tak dusro ki reels par engage karein. Isse initial push milta hai.
💡 Trick 5: Story Reach Hack
Din ki pehli story hamesha ek engaging Poll ya Quiz lagayein. Jitne zyada votes aayenge, aapki baaki saari stories ki reach automatic double ho jayegi.
